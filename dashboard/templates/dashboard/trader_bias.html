TradingView Trade Ideas

TradingView is a charting and trading information platform. TradingView can be used to place trades directly when linked with your exchange. It boasts a large community of active traders who exchange ideas and strategies regarding their trades. Under the ideas section you can see all recently published trade ideas for any given asset.

Pythons Beautifulsoup library was used to scrape for data about these trade ideas. This is a comparison of the amount of Long trade ideas (betting on the price going up) vs the amount of short trade ideas (betting on the price going down).

This can be used to gauge the sentiment of the traders regarding the price action of Bitcoin.
