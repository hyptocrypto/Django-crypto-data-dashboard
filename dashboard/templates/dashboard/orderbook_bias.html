San Francisco-based Kraken is one of the largest Bitcoin exchanges, and is considered the largest bitcoin exchange based on the euro volume and liquidity. Kraken landed in the international scene in 2011, and was the first Bitcoin exchange to have trading price and volume displayed in the Bloomberg Terminal. Kraken’s was also the first to pass a cryptographically verifiable proof-of-reserves audit, is the Tokyo government’s court-appointed trustee, and Germany’s BaFin regulated Fidor Bank.
